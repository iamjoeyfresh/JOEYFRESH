JoeyFresh


Welcome to my website. I have always been into music. I started Dancing when I was a Young Boy then Branched into the world of playing music when I got my first mixer/controller in 2015. I was then trained on vinyl when I started going out and Watching the Vynil Squad play. They continued my Journey into the world of music. after hanging around with them in the year of 2017 thats when I got my first set of turntables. I then continued that path learning to Mix, Battle DJ, MC, and Read the Crowd itself. I started playing various clubs in my area only to grow and expand to different variety of events. Now I play all types of events whether its club events, private events, BBQs, sweet sixteens, weddings...ect if you are in the need of music entertainment Then im your guy!!!
